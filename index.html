MULTITASK AND SAVE A LIFE

Hands Free Emergency is a mobile application designed to contact emergency services as quickly as possible while giving the user the opportunity to perform life saving actions with instructions given by the application.

  • Quickly send emergency services a text describing the situation
  • Sends your location to emergency services

    FEATURES

  • Videos and images in order to help the person in danger
  • Numpad verification to protect from children and random calls
  • Text-to-speech instructions
